Spam and Potatoes Recipe


Spam and potatoes is a simple, hearty dish featuring diced or sliced Spam pan-fried with potatoes until golden and crispy. Often served with homemade tortillas or biscuits, this dish is a popular comfort food known for its savory, slightly salty flavor.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium to large russet potatoes, peeled and sliced into 1/8 in slices about 2 inches in width
  • 1 half can of SPAM diced into cubes
  • 2 teaspoons of olive oil
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a medium sized skillet heat pan on medium high and add olive oil, salt, and pepper to pan
  2. Slowly add in sliced potatoes and stir, let cook for 10 minutes on medium high
  3. Add in SPAM and cook for an additional 10 minutes or until potatoes are soft
  4. Serve with biscuits or tortillas!
